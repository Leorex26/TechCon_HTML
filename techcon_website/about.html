Our History

TechCon was established in 2010 with the goal of creating a platform for technology enthusiasts, industry leaders, and innovators to come together and share their insights. Over the years, TechCon has grown into one of the most anticipated technology conferences worldwide.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to foster innovation, connect the brightest minds in the industry, and inspire the next generation of tech leaders. We aim to create a space where ideas can flourish and partnerships can thrive.

Notable Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has had the honor of hosting some of the most influential figures in technology and innovation:

  • Dr. Ada Lovelace: Pioneer of computer science and advocate for women in technology.

  • Mr. Alan Turing: Mathematician and visionary behind artificial intelligence.

  • Ms. Grace Hopper: Developer of the first compiler and tech educator.
